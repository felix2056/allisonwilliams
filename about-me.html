Allison C. Williams

I am the Founder and Managing Partner of Williams Law Group, LLC, a family law firm serving clients across New Jersey with offices in Parsippany, Short Hills, Tinton Falls, and Cranford. My practice focuses exclusively on matrimonial and family law, with a particular emphasis on complex child welfare matters such as child abuse and neglect cases, termination of parental rights, and consultations related to child abuse investigations.

I represent clients in courts across 16 counties as well as the Office of Administrative Law, and I am also an international speaker and national CLE provider who regularly educates judges, lawyers, and paralegals on child welfare law. Before entering private practice, I served as a Judicial Law Clerk to the Honorable James A. Farber, J.S.C., in the Superior Court of New Jersey, Chancery Division – Family Part.
